About Me

Hi, I'm Navi. I'm 21 years old and currently rebuilding my life from the ground up. Right now I own a phone, a laptop, my IDs, and about $6 in my Chime account. I don't come from wealth, I don't have a network of influential people, I don't have a college degree or certifications, and I've only worked a few short-term jobs. This project exists to document what happens when someone starts with almost nothing and simply refuses to quit. Every day I work toward becoming better than I was yesterday—learning new skills, building projects, creating opportunities, and sharing the journey publicly. I don't expect anyone to rescue me. I don't ask people for money. If someone chooses to support the journey, I'm incredibly grateful—but my goal has always been to build a future through consistency, hard work, and transparency.
